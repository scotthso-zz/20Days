Why I love Rap

by Scott So

May 14th 2014

Rap has always been a controversal topic.


However, that is mainly due to one genre of hip hop music, which is "Gangsta rap."

Gangsta rap is a subgenre of hip hop music popularized by rap groups such as N.W.A. The lyrics were extremely controversal as they promoted crime, violence, sex, racism, drugs and many more.
The reason it became so popular was because it was so commericially lucrative.

As time went on, the style and flow of Gangsta rap became less hardcore and more laid back. R&B hooks became more popular but the lyrics were still promoting money, sex and drugs.

People also find Rap to be lame and talentless, as all people do is talk over a beat. However, rap is much more than that.

Rap involves complicated rhyming schemes and clever play on words. It's also a whole community with songs that insult other rappers or tell a story.

Popular yet controrversial website rapgenius.com has helped listeners gain a better insight on rappers and their lyrics.
